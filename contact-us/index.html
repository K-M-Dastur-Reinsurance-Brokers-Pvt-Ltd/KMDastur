Compliance

Every employee is trained to adhere to the highest ethical standards and at the same time to conform to the business requirements of the regulators.

Professional procedures are followed to ensure that Client data is stored, managed and used in a secure and confidential manner and records are maintained as such. Regular audits by experts in the area of compliance are made to ensure that departments and individuals are being constantly trained to reduce any opportunity for error. At our headquarters, Deloitte & Touche conduct an internal & systems audit. Our headquarter is awarded an ISO 9001: 2015 certification from TUV, NORD since 2005. (In addition, we have audits conducted by TUV, NORD annually)

We are grateful to all our customers for their trust, co-operation and support.

If you have any suggestions, queries or complaints, do please write in and we will get back to you.
